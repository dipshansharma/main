Essential Packing: Nepal to UK Reality Check

Preparing for life in the UK as a student from Nepal requires balancing your 30kg baggage allowance with the unique climate of the British Isles. Here is the expert logic on what to bring and what to buy locally.

Pack These (Nepal-Exclusive)

  • Cultural Attire: Dashain and Tihar events at Uni are huge. Bring your Kurta or Daura Suruwal—UK prices for these are 5x higher.
  • High-Altitude Spices: Authentic Timur and Momo masala are student "gold." Most other spices are cheap at local Asian grocers.
  • Quality Thermals: Kathmandu's thermal wear is perfect for the UK's damp, penetrating cold.

Buy These in the UK

  • Heavy Winter Coats: UK wind is wet. Buy a waterproof parka from **Vinted** or **Primark** once you land.
  • Bedding & Kitchen: Go to **B&M** or **Poundland**. A student kitchen set costs less than £20.
  • Bulky Rice/Dals: Save your baggage weight. 10kg bags of Basmati are cheap at UK supermarkets.

Working Legally in the UK

The 20-Hour Rule

During term time, you are legally capped at **20 hours per week**. Working over this is a serious visa violation. You can work full-time only during official University holidays.
